Registration Start 15th Januanry 2021

We’re accepting applications from techies, individuals and schools. It will take place from January 15th - April 30th 2021.

1. If you want to apply, please submit your application online by 8 pm PTon January 15th. Inventors that submit early have a small advantage because we have more time to read their applications. And you can submit after the deadline - though keep in mind that if you apply late, we can’t guarantee the exact date of when you’ll hear back from us with a decision.
2. On April 27th by 11:59PM PST, we’ll invite the startups that seem most promising to talk to us either in late May, June, or early July.
3. This year’s National challenge will be remote due to COVID-19. It will be much the same as every other batch. .
4. During the 3 month cycle we invite speakers every week to talk about their startup and what they learned.
5. Every week or two, you’ll have group office hours, where you’ll talk with 5-8 companies and your YC partners for an hour. These group office hours are a chance to get to know companies in your batch that are going through similar things and to help each other.
